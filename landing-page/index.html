Will this fit my motorcycle?
+

Yes! Universal M8 thread screws fit 99% of motorcycles, scooters, and electric bikes in Nepal. Just check your current turn signals use M8 mounting.

How do I install them?
+

Installation takes 30 seconds! Remove old turn signals, connect 3 wires (yellow for steering, black for ground, red for power), and screw in the M8 bolts. No special tools needed.

Is it really waterproof?
+

Absolutely! IP67 rated — fully protected against dust and can withstand heavy rain, splashes, and even temporary submersion. Perfect for Nepal's monsoon season.

How long does the battery last?
+

These run on your bike's 12V system, so they work as long as your bike is running. Only 6.6W per bulb — won't drain your battery at all.
